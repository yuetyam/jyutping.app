Jyutping Keyboard for Android





A. Install Jyutping Keyboard from Google Play

Google Play is the official Android app store.
Installing Jyutping from Play Store has several advantages:

  1. Complete features, stable experience, and good compatibility.
  2. Strong safety checks and a straightforward installation process.
  3. Easy updates to the latest version and latest features.
Search for 粵拼 or Jyutping to find Jyutping Keyboard.


B. Install and update Jyutping Keyboard with F-Droid

If you cannot use Google Play for any reason, we strongly recommend installing and updating Jyutping Keyboard with F-Droid.

In short, F-Droid is like an open-source version of Google Play, focused on free and open-source software.

First install the F-Droid app from its official website: https://f-droid.org
You can also download its APK from here: fdroid_v2.0-alpha9_2000009.apk
